Item 5.07    Submission of Matters to a Vote of Security Holders.
At the annual meeting of stockholders (the "Annual Meeting") of Intel Corporation (the "Company") held on May 6, 2025, a total of 3,328,455,208 shares of the Company's common stock were present or represented by proxy, representing 76.3% of the 4,360,591,639 shares outstanding as of the close of business on March 10, 2025, the record date for the determination of stockholders entitled to vote at the Annual Meeting.
