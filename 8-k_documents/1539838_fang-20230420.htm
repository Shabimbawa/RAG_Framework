Item 2.02. Results of Operations and Financial Condition.
 
Diamondback Energy, Inc. (“Diamondback” or the “Company”) presents in this Item 2.02 certain information for the quarter ended March 31, 2023 regarding its derivative activity, realized prices, and weighted average basic and diluted shares outstanding.

Realized Prices

First quarter 2023 average unhedged realized prices were $73.11 per barrel of oil, $1.46 per Mcf of natural gas and $23.16 per barrel of natural gas liquids ("NGLs"), resulting in a total equivalent unhedged realized price of $49.72 per BOE.

First quarter 2023 average realized hedged prices were $72.05 per barrel of oil, $1.96 per Mcf of natural gas and $23.16 per barrel of NGLs, resulting in a total equivalent hedged price of $49.72 per BOE.
