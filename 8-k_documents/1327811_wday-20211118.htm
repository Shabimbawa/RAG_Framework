Item 8.01 – Other Events
On November 18, 2021, Workday, Inc. (“Workday”) issued a press release announcing its entry into a definitive agreement (the “Merger Agreement”) by and among Workday, VNDLY, Inc. (“VNDLY”), and certain other parties thereto. Pursuant to the Merger Agreement, Workday will, subject to the satisfaction of certain conditions and receipt of required regulatory approvals, acquire all of the outstanding capital stock of VNDLY. A copy of the press release is attached hereto as Exhibit 99.1.
