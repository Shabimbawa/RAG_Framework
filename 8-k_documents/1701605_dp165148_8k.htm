Item 5.02 Departure of Directors or Certain Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Certain Officers; Compensatory Arrangements of Certain Officers.

 

Uwem Ukpong, Executive Vice President, Regions, Alliances and Enterprise Sales of Baker Hughes Company (the “Company”), will depart from the Company effective January 31, 2022 (“Separation Date”).

 

The Company has entered into a letter agreement with Mr. Ukpong, dated January 12, 2022, pursuant to which Mr. Ukpong and the Company mutually agreed as to the terms of his departure. Pursuant to this letter agreement, provided Mr. Ukpong (i) executes a release of claims against the Company and (ii) provides transition and consulting services for 90 days following the Separation Date as required by the Company in order to smoothly transition his duties to his successor and other employees of the Company, the Company will pay Mr. Ukpong $780,000. In addition, Mr. Ukpong acknowledged and affirmed (A) he will not be entitled to severance benefits under the Company’s Executive Severance Program or any other program or agreement, (B) his outstanding long-term incentive awards will be governed by the terms of the applicable award agreements and long-term incentive programs and (C) certain contractual obligations he has to the Company, including confidentiality, non-competition and non-solicitation, will remain in effect for specified periods following his employment.

 

The foregoing description of the letter agreement does not purport to be complete and is qualified in its entirety by reference to the text of the letter agreement, which is furnished with this Form 8-K as Exhibit 10.1.
