Item 8.01.Other Events.

 

PepsiCo Senior Notes Offering.

 

On October 5, 2020, PepsiCo, Inc. (“PepsiCo”) announced an offering of €750,000,000 aggregate principal amount of its 0.400% Senior Notes due 2032 (the “2032 Notes”) and €750,000,000 aggregate principal amount of its 1.050% Senior Notes due 2050 (the “2050 Notes,” and together with the 2032 Notes, the “Notes”). Deutsche Bank AG, London Branch, Goldman Sachs & Co. LLC and Merrill Lynch International were joint book-running managers for the offering of the Notes.

 

PepsiCo received net proceeds of approximately €1,489 million, after deducting underwriting discounts and estimated offering expenses payable by PepsiCo. The net proceeds will be used for general corporate purposes, including the repayment of commercial paper.

 

The Notes were offered and sold pursuant to a Terms Agreement (the “Terms Agreement”) dated October 5, 2020 (incorporating the PepsiCo, Inc. Underwriting Agreement Standard Provisions dated as of November 18, 2019 (the “Standard Provisions”)) among PepsiCo and the several underwriters, under PepsiCo’s automatic shelf registration statement (the “Registration Statement”) on Form S-3 (File No. 333-234767), filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission (the “SEC”) on November 18, 2019. PepsiCo has filed with the SEC a prospectus supplement, dated October 5, 2020, together with the accompanying prospectus, dated November 18, 2019, relating to the offer and sale of the Notes. The Notes were issued on October 9, 2020 pursuant to an Indenture (the “Indenture”) dated as of May 21, 2007 between PepsiCo and The Bank of New York Mellon, as Trustee. The following table summarizes information about the Notes and the offering thereof.

 

Title of Securities:   0.400% Senior Notes due 2032   1.050% Senior Notes due 2050
Aggregate Principal Amount Offered:   €750,000,000   €750,000,000
Maturity Date:   October 9, 2032   October 9, 2050
Interest Payment Dates:   Annually on October 9, commencing October 9, 2021.   Annually on October 9, commencing October 9, 2021.
Coupon:   0.400%   1.050%
Optional Redemption:   Prior to July 9, 2032, make-whole call at comparable government bond rate plus 15 basis points; par call at any time on or after July 9, 2032.   Prior to April 9, 2050, make-whole call at comparable government bond rate plus 20 basis points; par call at any time on or after April 9, 2050.
Price to Public:   99.860%   99.821%

 

The Notes are unsecured obligations of PepsiCo and rank equally with all of PepsiCo’s other unsecured senior indebtedness. The Indenture also contains customary event of default provisions.
