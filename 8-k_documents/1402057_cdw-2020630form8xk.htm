CDW Corporation (the "Company") is furnishing under cover of this Current Report on Form 8-K a copy of its press release dated August 5, 2020 announcing its second quarter 2020 financial results. Quarterly Cash Dividend

A copy of the press release announcing the declaration of the quarterly cash dividend is attached to this report as Exhibit 99.2 and incorporated herein by reference.
