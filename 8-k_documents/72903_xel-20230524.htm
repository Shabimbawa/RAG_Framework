Item 5.07. Submission of Matters to a Vote of Security Holders.

Xcel Energy Inc. ("Xcel Energy") held its 2023 Annual Meeting of Shareholders on May 24, 2023. At the meeting, shareholders:

•elected all 12 directors nominated by the Board of Directors;
•approved, on an advisory basis, Xcel Energy's executive compensation as set forth in the 2023 proxy statement;
•approved, on an advisory basis, a frequency of 1 year for Xcel Energy's say on pay votes; and
•ratified the appointment of Deloitte & Touche LLP as Xcel Energy's independent registered public accounting firm for 2023.
