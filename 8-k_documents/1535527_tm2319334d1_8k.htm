Item 5.07Submission of Matters to a Vote of Security Holders.

 

CrowdStrike Holdings, Inc. (the “Company”) held its Annual Meeting of Stockholders on June 21, 2023 (the “Annual Meeting”). Proxies for the Annual Meeting were solicited pursuant to Regulation 14A of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, as amended. At the close of business on April 28, 2023, the record date for the Annual Meeting, there were 224,123,381 shares of Class A common stock outstanding and entitled to vote and 12,975,938 shares of Class B common stock outstanding and entitled to vote. Holders of Class A common stock were entitled to one vote per share on each proposal. Holders of Class B common stock were entitled to 10 votes per share on each proposal.

 

At the Annual Meeting, the Company’s stockholders voted on the following two proposals, each of which is described in more detail in the Company’s Definitive Proxy Statement on Schedule 14A filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission on May 5, 2023. The number of votes cast with respect to each proposal was as indicated below:
