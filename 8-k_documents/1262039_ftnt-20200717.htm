Item 5.03 Amendments to Articles of Incorporation or Bylaws; Change in Fiscal Year.

On July 17, 2020, the Board of Directors (the “Board”) of Fortinet, Inc. (the “Company”) amended and restated the Company’s Bylaws (the “Amended and Restated Bylaws”) to adopt a provision allowing for stockholders of the Company holding not less than 25% of the Company’s outstanding shares to call for a special meeting of stockholders, subject to the restrictions and procedures in the Amended and Restated Bylaws.

The foregoing summary and description of the provisions of the Amended and Restated Bylaws does not purport to be complete and is qualified in its entirety by reference to the full text of the Amended and Restated Bylaws, a copy of which is filed as Exhibit 3.1 with this Current Report on Form 8-K and is incorporated herein by reference.

Item 8.01 Other Events.

On July 20, 2020, the Company announced its acquisition of OPAQ Networks, Inc. (“OPAQ”), a Secure Access Service Edge, or “SASE,” cloud provider based in Herndon, Virginia. The Company acquired OPAQ for approximately $13.0 million in cash consideration, $1.3 million of which is to be held in escrow.
