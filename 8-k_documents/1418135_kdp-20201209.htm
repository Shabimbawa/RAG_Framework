Item 5.02. Departure of Directors or Principal Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Principal Officers.

On December 9, 2020, Anna-Lena Kamenetzky notified the Chairman of the Board of Directors (the “Board”) of Keurig Dr Pepper Inc. (the “Company”) of her resignation from the Board. Ms. Kamenetzky’s resignation did not result from any disagreement with the Company or the Board.

Also on December 9, 2020, the Board appointed Justine Tan, a partner at JAB Holding Company, as a member of the Board, effective immediately. Ms. Tan will serve for an initial term to expire concurrently with the terms of the other members of the Board at the Company’s 2021 annual meeting of stockholders, or until her earlier death, resignation or removal. Ms. Tan will not serve on any committees of the Board. There are no arrangements or understandings between Ms. Tan and any other persons pursuant to which Ms. Tan was appointed as a director and Ms. Tan is not a party to any transaction with the Company reportable under Item 404(a) of Regulation S-K under the Securities Act of 1933. Ms. Tan will participate in the compensation arrangements for non-employee directors as described in the Company’s 2020 Proxy Statement filed with the SEC on April 29, 2020.

A copy of the Company’s press release announcing the director appointment is attached as Exhibit 99.1 to this Form 8-K.
