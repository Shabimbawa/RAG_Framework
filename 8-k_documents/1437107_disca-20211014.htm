Item 8.01.    Other Events.

(New York, NY) — October 14, 2021 — Discovery (Nasdaq: DISCA, DISCB, DISCK) today announced that it will report its third-quarter 2021 results on Wednesday, November 3, 2021, at 7:00 a.m. ET. The Company will host a conference call at 8:00 a.m. ET that same day to discuss the results. The replay can be accessed by phone by dialing 800-585-8367 or 416-621-4642 using conference ID 2085256.
