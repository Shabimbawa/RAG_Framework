Item 8.01.    Other Events.

In connection with the Agreement and Plan of Merger, dated as of May 17, 2021, as it may be amended from time to time (the “Merger Agreement”), by and among Discovery, Inc., ("Discovery"), Drake Subsidiary, Inc., a Delaware corporation and a wholly owned subsidiary of Discovery (“Merger Sub”), AT&T Inc., a Delaware corporation (“AT&T”), and Magallanes, Inc., a Delaware corporation and a wholly owned subsidiary of AT&T (“Spinco”), pursuant to which Discovery will acquire the WarnerMedia Business of AT&T, with the combined company to be renamed Warner Bros. Discovery, Inc. (“WBD”), on March 15, 2022, Discovery issued a press release announcing the full slate of board designees for WBD. The designees will be elected and begin serving on the WBD Board effective at the closing of the combination.
