Item 8.01. Other Events
On February 16, 2021, the Registrant announced that its Board of Directors declared a quarterly cash dividend of $0.69 per outstanding share of common stock, an increase from the previously paid dividend of $0.62 per outstanding share of common stock. The dividend will be paid on March 9, 2021 to all shareholders of record at the close of business on February 26, 2021. A copy of the Registrant's press release is attached as Exhibit 99.1 to this Current Report on Form 8-K and is incorporated herein by reference.
