Regulation FD Disclosure.

On March 11, 2021, T-Mobile US, Inc. (the “Company”) hosted an analyst day (“Analyst Day”). Members of the Company’s senior leadership team discussed the Company’s plans to unlock future growth and capture momentum over the next five years.
