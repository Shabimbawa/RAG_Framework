Item 8.01 Other Events.

NXP Semiconductors N.V. (the "Company") through its subsidiaries NXP B.V. and NXP Funding LLC, delivered notice on September 9, 2020 to the Trustee of its 4.125% Senior Notes due 2021 and 4.625% Senior Notes due 2022 that it will fully redeem on September 28, 2020, the $400 million of outstanding principal of the 4.625% Senior Notes due 2022 and the $1.35 billion of outstanding principal of the 4.125% Senior Notes due 2021 (the “Note Redemption”).

The Company will utilize cash on its balance sheet to fund the planned Note Redemption.

A copy of the Company's press release announcing the Note Redemption is attached as Exhibit 99.1 to this Current Report on Form 8-K, and is incorporated by reference herein.
