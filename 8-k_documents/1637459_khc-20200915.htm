Item 7.01. Regulation FD Disclosure.
On September 15, 2020, The Kraft Heinz Company (the “Company”) issued a press release announcing the entry into a definitive agreement to sell certain of the Company’s cheese businesses to a U.S. affiliate of Groupe Lactalis. Additional information regarding the proposed transaction, which is subject to regulatory review and approval, can be found in the Company’s press release. A copy of the press release is furnished as Exhibit 99.1 to this Current Report on Form 8-K.
