Item 8.01Other Events.

 

Annual Meeting

 

On May 2, 2022, Tesla, Inc. (the “Company” or “Tesla”) announced that it established a meeting date of August 4, 2022 for its upcoming 2022 Annual Meeting of Stockholders (the “Annual Meeting”) in Austin, TX. The corresponding record date for determining the stockholders of the Company entitled to notice of, and to vote at, the Annual Meeting, will be June 6, 2022.

The Company’s definitive proxy statement will include additional details relating to the Annual Meeting.
