Item 5.02. Departure of Directors or Certain Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Certain Officers; Compensatory Arrangements of Certain Officers

On December 20, 2021, Xcel Energy Inc. (“Xcel Energy”) announced that, effective March 1, 2022, Jeff Savage will become Xcel Energy’s Chief Audit and Financial Services Officer and will cease serving in his current role. Mr. Savage currently serves as Xcel Energy’s Senior Vice President, Controller and as principal accounting officer. Xcel Energy intends to appoint a new principal accounting officer prior to March 1, 2022.

Item 7.01. Regulation FD Disclosure.

On December 20, 2021, Xcel Energy issued a press release relating to the announcement described in Item 5.02 as well as other leadership changes. A copy of the press release is being furnished as Exhibit 99.01 to this Form 8-K.

The information (including Exhibit 99.01) being furnished pursuant to this “Item 7.01 Regulation FD Disclosure” shall not be deemed to be “filed” for the purposes of Section 18 of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, as amended (the “Exchange Act”), or otherwise subject to the liabilities of that section and shall not be deemed to be incorporated by reference into any filing under the Securities Act of 1933, as amended, or the Exchange Act regardless of any general incorporation language in such filing.
