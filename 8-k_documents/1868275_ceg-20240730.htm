Section 8 - Other Events
Item 8.01. Other Events

On July 30, 2024, Constellation Energy Corporation (the "Company") learned the results of the PJM capacity auction for the 2025-2026 planning year. Each of the Company's nuclear, natural gas, and oil generation power plants located in the PJM market cleared in the auction. Capacity revenues for nuclear units are included in the gross receipts calculation for the Production Tax Credit. The auction results take effect June 1, 2025.
