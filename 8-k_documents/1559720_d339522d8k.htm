Item 5.07. Submission of Matters to a Vote of Security Holders.

On June 1, 2022, Airbnb, Inc. (the “Company”) held its 2022 Annual Meeting of Stockholders (the “Annual Meeting”). Set forth below are the matters acted upon at the Annual Meeting and the final voting results on each matter.
