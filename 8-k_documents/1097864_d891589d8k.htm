Item 8.01.

Other Events.

On March 5, 2025, ON Semiconductor Corporation, a Delaware corporation (“onsemi” or the “Company”) issued a press release confirming that it delivered to the Board of Directors of Allegro MicroSystems, Inc. (“Allegro”) a proposal to acquire all of the outstanding common stock of Allegro for $35.10 per share in cash. A copy of the press release, attached hereto as Exhibit 99.1, is incorporated herein by reference.
