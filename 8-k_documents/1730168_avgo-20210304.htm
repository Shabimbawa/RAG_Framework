Item 2.02    Results of Operations and Financial Condition.
On March 4, 2021, Broadcom Inc. (“Broadcom” or the “Company”) issued a press release announcing its unaudited financial results for the first fiscal quarter ended January 31, 2021. The Company will host an investor conference call on March 4, 2021 at 2:00 p.m. Pacific Time to discuss these results.
The foregoing description is qualified in its entirety by reference to the press release dated March 4, 2021, a copy of which is attached hereto as Exhibit 99.1. 
Item 8.01     Other Events.
On March 4, 2021, the Company announced that the Board of Directors has declared a quarterly cash dividend on the Company’s 8.00% Mandatory Convertible Preferred Stock, Series A (the "Mandatory Convertible Preferred Stock"), of $20.00 per share. This dividend is payable on March 31, 2021 to Mandatory Convertible Preferred Stock holders of record at the close of business (5:00 p.m., Eastern Time) on March 15, 2021.
The Company also announced that the Board of Directors has declared a quarterly cash dividend on the Company's common stock of $3.60 per share. This dividend is payable on March 31, 2021 to common stockholders of record at the close of business (5:00 p.m., Eastern Time) on March 22, 2021.
