Item 8.01 Other Events.

On June 19, 2020, Synopsys, Inc. (“Synopsys”) issued a press release announcing that its board of directors had replenished Synopsys’ existing stock repurchase program up to $500 million. The program authorizes, but does not obligate, Synopsys to purchase up to $500 million of its common stock, and Synopsys’ chief financial officer or board of directors may suspend or terminate the program at any time. A copy of the press release is attached hereto as Exhibit 99.1 and is incorporated by reference herein.
