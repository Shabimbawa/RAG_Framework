Section 8 – Other Events
Item 8.01. Other Events

Exelon Corporation (Exelon) and Exelon Generation Company, LLC (Generation) announced on November 24, 2021 that, following successful settlement negotiations, they have entered into a Joint Proposal with the Staff of the New York State Department of Public Service, the New York State Office of the Attorney General and the Alliance for a Green Economy regarding the petition filed by Exelon and Generation with the New York State Public Service Commission (the Commission) requesting approval for Exelon’s previously announced plan to separate its utilities business and Generation, including Generation’s three nuclear power plants in New York. The Joint Proposal remains subject to approval by the Commission, and neither Exelon or Generation can provide assurance as to the timing of any approval or that the Commission will approve the Joint Proposal at all, on the terms set forth therein or with such modifications or additional terms that are acceptable to Exelon and Generation. A copy of the Joint Proposal is attached hereto as Exhibit 99.1.
