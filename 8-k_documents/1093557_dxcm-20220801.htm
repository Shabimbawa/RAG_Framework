ITEM 8.01.
OTHER EVENTS.
On August 1, 2022, DexCom, Inc. (the “Company”) entered into an accelerated share repurchase agreement (“ASR”) with JPMorgan Chase Bank, National Association (“JPMorgan”) to repurchase shares of its common stock, par value $0.001 per share (“Common Stock”) in an aggregate notional amount of up to $700.0 million. Under the ASR, the Company made an initial payment to JPMorgan of $700.0 million, and received an initial delivery of approximately 3.0 million shares of its Common Stock on August 3, 2022. The final number of shares to be repurchased and the final aggregate notional amount will be based on the daily average volume-weighted average price of the Company’s Common Stock during the term of the ASR, and in the case of the final number of shares, less a discount. The final settlement of the ASR is expected to occur during the third quarter of 2022.
The ASR transaction will be effectuated pursuant to the Company’s previously announced $700.0 million share repurchase program, with a repurchase period ending no later than June 30, 2023.
