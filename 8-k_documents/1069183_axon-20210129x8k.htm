Item 1.01 Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement

On January 29, 2021, Axon Enterprise, Inc. (the "Company" or "Axon"), entered into an Amendment to its Amended and Restated Credit Agreement (the "Credit Agreement") with JPMorgan Chase Bank, N.A. The amendment, which was effective January 25, 2021, extends the term of the Credit Agreement from December 31, 2021 to December 31, 2023 and increases the amount of the unsecured revolving line of credit which is available for letters of credit from $10 million to $20 million. The amendment also updates the amounts or terms of certain of the affirmative and negative covenants, and identifies certain events related to the anticipated cessation of the London Inter-Bank Offered Rate that would result in the selection of an alternate rate of interest.
