Item 8.01.

Other Events.

On May 18, 2023, Take-Two Interactive Software, Inc. (the “Company”) announced that it has commenced a tender offer to purchase for cash up to $500 million aggregate principal amount of the Company’s 3.300% Senior Notes due 2024. The tender offer is being conducted on the terms and conditions set forth in the offer to purchase dated May 18, 2023. A copy of the press release announcing the tender offer is attached hereto as Exhibit 99.1 and is incorporated in this Item 8.01 by reference.

The information contained in this Current Report on Form 8-K shall not constitute an offer to sell, or a solicitation of an offer to purchase, any securities in any jurisdiction in which such an offer, solicitation or sale would be unlawful.
