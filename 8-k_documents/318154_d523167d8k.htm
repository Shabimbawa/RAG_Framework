Item 8.01

Other Events

As previously disclosed, Amgen Inc. (“Amgen”), Pillartree Limited, a private limited company incorporated under the laws of Ireland and a wholly owned subsidiary of Amgen, and Horizon Therapeutics plc (“Horizon”) entered into that certain Transaction Agreement (the “Transaction Agreement”), dated as of December 11, 2022. On September 11, 2023, Amgen and Horizon jointly acknowledged that the First Extended End Date (as defined in the Transaction Agreement) has been automatically extended to the Second Extended End Date (i.e., December 12, 2023).

As also previously disclosed, on September 5, 2023, the Irish High Court set a court hearing for October 5, 2023 to consider Horizon’s application for sanction of the scheme of arrangement under Irish law.
