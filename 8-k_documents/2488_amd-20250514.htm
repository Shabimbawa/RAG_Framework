Item 5.07 Submission of Matters to a Vote of Security Holders.
On May 14, 2025, Advanced Micro Devices, Inc. (the "Company") held its 2025 Annual Meeting of Stockholders (the "Annual Meeting"). At the Annual Meeting, the Company’s stockholders voted on the following six proposals, each of which is described in detail in the Company’s definitive proxy statement filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission on March 28, 2025 (the "Proxy"):

Proposal No. 1: Election of Directors. All Directors were elected.

The Company's stockholders elected the eight director nominees listed below to serve on the Company’s Board of Directors:
