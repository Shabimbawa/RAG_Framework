Item 5.07

Submission of Matters to a Vote of Security Holders

The following proposals were submitted to the holders of Common Stock of Verisk Analytics, Inc. (the “Company”) for a vote at the 2023 Annual Meeting of Shareholders held on May 17, 2023:

 

  1.

The election of six members of the Board of Directors each to serve one-year terms;

 

  2.

The advisory, non-binding resolution to approve the compensation of the Company’s named executive officers (“say-on-pay”);

 

  3.

The advisory, non-binding resolution as to whether future “say-on-pay” votes should be held every one, two or three years (“say-on-pay frequency”); and

 

  4.

The ratification of the appointment of Deloitte & Touche LLP as the Company’s independent auditors for the year ending December 31, 2023.

The results of such votes were as follows:
