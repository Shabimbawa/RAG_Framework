Item 8.01. Other Events.

Analog Devices, Inc. (the “Company”) gave notice today that it will redeem for cash all of its outstanding 2.950% Senior Notes due January 12, 2021 (the “Notes”) in accordance with the terms of the indenture governing the Notes. The redemption date for the outstanding Notes will be September 25, 2020 (the “Redemption Date”) and the redemption price will be equal to the greater of: (a) 100% of the principal amount of the Notes, and (b) the sum of the present values of the remaining scheduled payments on the Notes, discounted to the Redemption Date on a semi-annual basis (assuming a 360-day year consisting of twelve 30-day months) at the Treasury Rate plus 12.5 basis points, plus accrued and unpaid interest up to (but not including) the Redemption Date. As of September 9, 2020, approximately $450 million aggregate principal amount of the Notes is outstanding.
