Item 1.01 Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement.
Effective April 29, 2025, Zscaler, Inc. (the "Company") entered into a sublease between the Company and Airbnb, Inc, dated April 24, 2025 (the "Sublease"). The effectiveness of the Sublease was conditioned upon obtaining the consent of the landlord to the Sublease.
The properties subject to the Sublease are located at 4301 and 4401 Great America Parkway, Santa Clara, California and consist of up to an aggregate of approximately 301,000 square feet of rentable space.
The term of the Sublease commences on September 1, 2026 and terminates on April 30, 2032. The total base rent, including committed fixed expenses through the duration of the Sublease term, is approximately $69.5 million. In addition to the base rent, the Company will be responsible for covering operating costs and other related expenses.
The above description of the Sublease does not purport to be complete and is qualified in its entirety by reference to the Sublease, which will be filed as an exhibit to the Company’s Quarterly Report on Form 10-Q for the three months ended April 30, 2025.
