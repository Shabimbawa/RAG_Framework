Item 7.01Regulation FD Disclosure.

 

On August 16, 2023, Gilead Sciences, Inc., a Delaware corporation (the “Company”), announced that the International Association for the Study of Lung Cancer (“IASLC”) inadvertently posted the following presentation on the IASLC’s 2023 World Conference on Lung Cancer website on the morning of August 16, 2023:

 

-“Sacituzumab Govitecan + Pembrolizumab in 1L Metastatic Non-Small Cell Lung Cancer: Preliminary Results of the EVOKE-02 Study”

 

Such posting by IASLC, ahead of the Company’s planned disclosure in connection with the conference, was not authorized by the Company. The presentation is attached as Exhibit 99.1 hereto and incorporated herein by reference.

 

The information in Item 7.01 shall not be deemed “filed” for purposes of Section 18 of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, as amended (the “Exchange Act”), or otherwise subject to the liabilities under that section, nor shall it be deemed incorporated by reference in any filing under the Securities Act of 1933, as amended, or the Exchange Act, regardless of any general incorporation language in such filing.
